About Our Project

We chose to participate in the "Hey, What Are You Looking At" challenge. We created a very simple web app that collects HEASARC and NEOSSat data from NASA and the Canadian Space Agency, and displays the observed objects on an interactive "sky map." Users can click on these objects, learn more information about them, and download the retrieved FITS image file.
